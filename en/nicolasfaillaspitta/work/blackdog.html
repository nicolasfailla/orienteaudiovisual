BLACK DOG
2017 / 15 min / 2K / Color / Stereo

Documentary

Director: Tomás Faiman
Producer: Juan José Pereira
Field Recording, Sound Design, Music and Mix: Nicolás Failla
Director Assistant: Agustín Pereyra
DOP: Lautaro Sostillo
Editing: Matías González, Lautaro Sostillo y Tomás Faiman

Synopsis:
The myth with images and metaphors, as it happens in dreams, expresses and reflects, organizes and legitimizes the totality of the culture of human groups. The myth traces, by obscure paths, the process of shaping the vision of the world that drives human collectivities to dominate their space, manipulate their time and perform ritualized celebrations to build their identity distinguishing them from the Other.

RAFMA Prize. Red Argentina de Festivales y Muestras Audiovisuales en Festival Internacional de Cine Independiente de Cosquín, Argentina, 2018
