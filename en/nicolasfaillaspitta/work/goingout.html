GOING OUT
2017 / 21 min / 2K / Color / Stereo

Short Film

Directors: Lautaro Sostillo y Matías Gonzalez
Producer: Juan José Pereira
Field Recording, Sound Design, Mix: Nicolás Failla

When night falls on Buenos Aires, Miguel goes lonely in search of fun and meets two foreign girls. Between lights and electronic music, the night life will gain another intensity.
