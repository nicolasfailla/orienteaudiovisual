THE INHABITED HOUSE

VR 360
2019 / / Color / Ambisonic 360| Mix

DIRECTED BY
Diego Kompel

AMBISONIC SOUND MIX
Nicolás Failla Spitta

Synopsis:
The inhabited house emerges as a necessity of working with my grandparents' house as a starting point. A space where I spent most of my childhood and that means a lot to me nowadays. It also emerges as a nostalgic desire to return in time, to happy childhoods, to see my grandfather and his father and parents as young people.
The immersive format operates effectively in the spatial plane and can transport the subject with just a 360º photo. The inhabited house proposes, as a starting point for this image overlay technique, to open a window between temporal dimensions, making the past and present coexist with each other.
The work also represents a close connection with the camera operator of the observed footage. Reflects on his point of view and on the cut that my father decided to make so many years ago.
The inhabited house is a documentary work and personal search. However, it tries to draw a line with the family and past paradigms. A reflection of what was and what is.
