REEL


We are a studio that specializes in the integral conception of sound for audiovisual products. We understand the sound design of a work as a complex and organic process. It requires both creative and technical planning to achieve the desired aesthetic proposal.


That is why we take care of all the stages of the sound language. We start with the conceptual sound design of the project. We do field recording and then the post-production with dialogue editing, ambience, FX, foley and dubbing (ADR). We make music composition and recording. Finally we do stereo mixing, 5.1 mix or 360° ambisonic sound according to the clients need.


We work for film, TV, advertising, videogames, VR (Virtual Reality) and installations. We record, produce and mix for bands and musicians . With our associated Media Production Company we can film your recording live to make a videoclip or reel.

We have a recording and mixing studio and all the necessary equipment to achieve a professional quality result, and according to distribution standards. We invite you to see our reel and portfolio to get to know the work we make.
